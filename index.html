About Me

Hi, I'm Asif Ullah (Asif Khan), an enthusiastic ASP.NET Core Full Stack Developer with a passion for building modern, scalable, efficient web applications, and ERPs. I enjoy turning complex problems into clean, user-friendly solutions and delivering high-quality software that makes a difference.

On the front-end, I have hands-on experience with jQuery, Angular 14, and JavaScript, allowing me to create dynamic and responsive user interfaces. On the back-end, I specialize in ASP.NET Core, developing robust APIs and managing data with SQL Server.

My goal is to combine my technical expertise and problem-solving skills to build applications that are not only functional but also scalable and maintainable. I thrive in collaborative environments, enjoy learning new technologies, and am always looking for opportunities to create innovative software solutions.
